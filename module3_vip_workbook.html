Subtopic 1.3.1

Comprensión auditiva de órdenes y solicitudes con distintos acentos en inglés

Students listen to realistic restaurant exchanges, identify key order details, and respond accurately to requests from different English-speaking voices.
Photo-Based Context

Observe the restaurant scene first. Predict what customers may order, what details the waiter must hear clearly, and what extra requests could appear in a busy dining room.

Service in Action

This subtopic is built around the restaurant photograph: a waiter taking notes, a couple making requests, and several tables speaking at the same time. Students use the scene to prepare for realistic listening tasks with varied voices and service needs.

  • Identify the main dish, drink, and table request.
  • Listen for polite variations such as Could I have..., Can I get..., and I'd like....
  • Notice modifications like no sugar, without onions, or sparkling water.
  • Practice confirming the order before the waiter leaves the table.

Subtopic 1.3.2

Active Listening in Real-Life Settings

Students show attentive listening through clarification, confirmation, and accurate response.
Focus
  • Listen carefully before answering.
  • Use confirmation questions to avoid mistakes.
  • Respond politely and professionally.
Suggested Activities
  1. Listen to short restaurant situations and identify what the guest needs.
  2. Repeat the request using your own words.
  3. Practice clarification phrases in pairs.

Subtopic 1.3.3

Integration of Skills in the Final Food Service Simulation

Students combine listening, speaking, and service vocabulary in a complete restaurant performance.
Focus
  • Integrate greeting, ordering, recommendations, and problem solving.
  • Use practical food-service English in sequence.
  • Demonstrate fluency and confidence in role play.
Suggested Activities
  1. Prepare a full restaurant interaction in small groups.
  2. Assign roles: waiter, customer, support staff, or cashier.
  3. Perform the complete scene and rotate roles.

Subtopic 1.3.4

Individual Practical Assessment

Students demonstrate personal performance through guided individual speaking and listening tasks.
Focus
  • Evaluate each student individually.
  • Measure listening comprehension and oral response.
  • Observe service language use in an authentic task.
Suggested Activities
  1. Listen to one short scenario and answer key questions.
  2. Respond to a customer request orally.
  3. Complete a mini service role play with the teacher.
