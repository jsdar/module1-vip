INSTRUCTIONS

Read each section carefully and complete all the activities. Use English in all answers. This exam evaluates reading, writing, listening, and speaking.

Part 1 - Reading (25 points)

Greetings and Basic Service

A. Read the dialogue and answer the questions. (10 points)

Waiter: Good evening. Welcome to our restaurant.

Guest: Good evening. Thank you.

Waiter: Do you have a reservation?

Guest: No, we don't.

Waiter: No problem. Please follow me to your table.

Waiter: Here is the menu. Would you like something to drink?

Guest: Yes, please. I'd like orange juice.

1. What does the waiter say first?

  1. a) Here is your bill.
  2. b) Good evening. Welcome to our restaurant.
  3. c) Are you ready to order?

2. Does the guest have a reservation?

  1. a) Yes
  2. b) No
  3. c) Maybe
B. Match the expressions with their meaning. (5 points)

1. Please follow me

2. Would you like something to drink?

3. Here is the menu

C. Read the mini menu and answer. (10 points)

Starters: Tomato Soup, Green Salad

Main Dishes: Grilled Chicken with Rice, Fish with Vegetables, Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Drinks: Water, Orange Juice, Soda

Desserts: Chocolate Cake, Ice Cream

Part 2 - Writing (25 points)

Courtesy and Vocabulary

A. Complete the dialogue. (10 points)

Word Bank: welcome - table - drink - order - thank

Waiter: Good evening. __________ to our restaurant.

Guest: Good evening.

Waiter: Please follow me to your __________.

Waiter: Would you like something to __________?

Waiter: Are you ready to __________?

Waiter: __________ you very much.

B. Write 5 polite expressions a waiter can use. (5 points)
C. Write a short restaurant dialogue. (10 points)

Include: greeting, offering the menu, taking a drink order, and saying thank you.

Part 3 - Listening (25 points)

Comprehension of Service Steps

A. Listen and complete. (10 points)

1. Good __________.

2. Welcome to our __________.

3. Please __________ me to your table.

4. Here is the __________.

5. I'd like __________, please.

B. Listen and choose the correct answer. (10 points)

What food does the guest order?

  1. a) Fish
  2. b) Grilled chicken
  3. c) Pasta

Is the guest ready to order?

  1. a) Yes
  2. b) No
C. Listen and write True or False. (5 points)

Part 4 - Speaking (25 points)

Simple Role Play

  • Greet the guest.
  • Offer a table and the menu.
  • Ask for a drink.
  • Take one simple order and say thank you politely.

20% Attendance and Participation

Observation Checklist

  • Participates in greeting drills and short oral interaction.
  • Uses polite expressions during class practice.
  • Shows readiness and consistent engagement.
40% Practical Workshops and Simulation

Module 1 Practical Assessment

  1. Role play a greeting and seating situation.
  2. Identify food, service, and courtesy words from a short listening prompt.
  3. Arrange the sequence of service from greeting to bill.
  4. Describe one dish using basic adjectives.

20% Virtual Activities

Online Evidence

  • Submit one short audio greeting.
  • Complete one matching or vocabulary activity online.
  • Upload one short written task with food and service words.
20% Final Theoretical Exam

Written Quiz

  • Multiple choice on greetings and restaurant protocol.
  • Fill in the blanks with key expressions.
  • Identify vocabulary and order basic service steps.
