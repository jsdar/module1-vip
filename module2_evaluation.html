INSTRUCTIONS

Read each section carefully and complete all the activities. Use English in all answers. This exam evaluates reading, writing, listening, and speaking through restaurant interaction.

Part 1 - Reading (25 points)

Order Taking and Suggestions

A. Read the dialogue and answer. (10 points)

Waiter: Are you ready to order?

Customer: Not yet. What do you recommend?

Waiter: I recommend the grilled fish. It is very fresh and delicious.

Customer: What comes with it?

Waiter: It comes with salad and rice.

Waiter: We also have today's special: beef soup.

1. What dish does the waiter recommend?

2. What is the special of the day?

3. Write two adjectives from the dialogue.

B. Match the expression with its function. (5 points)

1. I recommend the grilled fish.

2. What comes with it?

3. Great choice!

C. Read the group order and answer. (10 points)

Customer 1 orders a burger and fries with soda.

Customer 2 orders salad and still water.

Customer 3 orders pizza and juice.

Part 2 - Writing (25 points)

Restaurant Interaction

A. Complete the dialogue. (10 points)

Word Bank: recommend - drink - order - sorry - special

Waiter: Are you ready to __________?

Customer: What do you __________?

Waiter: Today's __________ is beef soup.

Waiter: Would you like something to __________?

Waiter: I'm __________ for the delay.

B. Write two recommendations. (5 points)
C. Write one apology and one solution. (5 points)
D. Write a short group order. (5 points)
Part 3 - Listening (25 points)

Service Comprehension

A. Listen and identify the details. (10 points)

1. Recommended dish: __________

2. Drink ordered: __________

3. Problem in the dialogue: __________

B. Listen and choose the correct answer. (10 points)

What does the waiter say to solve the problem?

  1. a) I will fix it right away.
  2. b) Please pay now.
  3. c) The kitchen is closed.

How many people are in the group order?

  1. a) Two
  2. b) Three
  3. c) Five
C. Listen and write one final order. (5 points)
