Student Evaluation Format

This module also presents a student-facing exam layout similar to the PDF, but adapted to listening comprehension and final integrated evaluation.

Student Name: ____________________________________
Date: ____________________________________
Course: English A1 for Waiters - Module 3
Teacher: ____________________________________
Score: __________ / 100
Part 1 - Reading (25 points)

Listening Support Texts

  1. Read a short service transcript and answer comprehension questions.
  2. Identify the request, the confirmation phrase, and the solution.
  3. Match active listening expressions with their purpose.
Part 2 - Writing (25 points)

Confirmation and Response

  1. Write two confirmation sentences using So you would like...
  2. Write one polite clarification question.
  3. Write a short reflection about a listening task or simulation.
Part 3 - Listening (25 points)

Orders and Requests

  1. Listen and note the food, drink, and special request.
  2. Identify whether the service response is correct or incorrect.
  3. Write True or False about a practical listening scenario.
Audio space for Listening Exam

Reserved for accent-based prompts, request recordings, or evaluation audio.

Part 4 - Speaking (25 points)

Individual Practical Task

  • Listen to one customer request.
  • Repeat the key information correctly.
  • Respond as the waiter using one confirmation phrase and one polite expression.
20% Attendance and Participation

Listening Participation Record

  • Completes listening warm-ups and note-taking routines.
  • Asks for clarification when needed.
  • Participates actively in feedback and reflection.
40% Practical Workshops and Simulation

Module 3 Performance Tasks

  1. Listen to a request with a different accent and note the key details.
  2. Respond orally using one confirmation phrase.
  3. Complete one integrated service simulation.
  4. Perform one individual practical response task.
Audio space for Practical Assessment

Reserved for listening prompts, oral instructions, or individual assessment clips.

20% Virtual Activities

Online Evidence

  • Upload one listening log or response sheet.
  • Submit one voice note with a polite confirmation response.
  • Complete one asynchronous reflection or self-assessment.
20% Final Theoretical Exam

Written Quiz

  • Answer transcript-based comprehension questions.
  • Identify the best response to a service situation.
  • Recognize listening strategies and service vocabulary.
