Part 1 - Reading (25 points)

Restaurant Communication Review

A. Read the dialogue and answer the questions. (10 points)

Waiter: Good evening. Welcome to our restaurant.

Customer: Good evening. I would like a table for two, please.

Waiter: Of course. Here is the menu. Today's special is grilled fish with rice.

Customer: That sounds good. I would like the fish and a lemonade with no sugar, please.

Waiter: Perfect. So you would like grilled fish with rice and one lemonade with no sugar, correct?

1. What greeting does the waiter use?

2. What is the special of the day?

3. What special request does the customer make?

B. Match the expression with its function. (5 points)

1. Good evening.

2. I recommend the grilled fish.

3. I'm very sorry.

4. So you would like...

C. Read the order note and answer. (10 points)

Table 4: one burger and fries, one salad, two still waters, one juice, allergy: peanuts.

1. How many drinks are in the order?

2. Which food item must be prepared carefully?

3. Write one sentence to confirm the order.

Part 2 - Writing (25 points)

Service Writing

A. Complete the dialogue. (10 points)

Word bank: menu, would like, recommend, sorry, confirm

B. Write one recommendation and one apology. (5 points)
C. Write a short waiter response. (10 points)

Include one greeting, one order confirmation, and one polite closing sentence.

Part 3 - Listening (25 points)

Orders, Requests, and Solutions

A. Listen and note the key details. (10 points)

Greeting: __________

Food: __________

Drink: __________

Special request or problem: __________

B. Listen and choose the correct answer. (10 points)

What should the waiter do after hearing the order?

  1. a) Repeat the order to confirm it.
  2. b) Give the bill.
  3. c) Leave the table.

  1. a) I recommend the soup.
  2. b) No problem table.
  3. c) Menu please bill.
