Part 1 - Reading (25 points)

Listening Support Texts

A. Read the service transcript. (10 points)

Guest: Excuse me, I would like fish and still water, please.

Waiter: So you would like fish and still water, correct?

Guest: Yes, that's right.

Waiter: Thank you. I will bring your order shortly.

1. What food does the guest order?

2. What confirmation phrase does the waiter use?

3. Why is confirmation important?

B. Match the active listening expression with its purpose. (5 points)

1. Could you repeat that?

2. So you would like...

3. Let me confirm

Part 2 - Writing (25 points)

Confirmation and Response

A. Write two confirmation sentences. (10 points)

Use: So you would like...

B. Write one polite clarification question. (5 points)
C. Write a short response to a customer request. (10 points)

Include one polite expression and one confirmation phrase.
