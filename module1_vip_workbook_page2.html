How to use this page

Students can solve these extra tasks after finishing page 1. The activities stay brief, visual and iframe-friendly so you can keep the same classroom flow.

Included practice

Each lesson adds one quick fill-in activity and one mini word search. Every exercise also has an empty block ready for audio insertion.
